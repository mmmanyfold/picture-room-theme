Natalie Weinberger, b. 1985, is a Brooklyn-based studio potter.

We are pleased to announce Natalie Weinberger as our Featured Artist for the months of May and June. BIG FOOT is a small collection of experimental, one of a kind ceramic objects celebrating the art, history & humor of the pedestaled vessel. The collection covers ornate chalices, delicate stemware, precarious vases and a range of other functional tabletop items, ranging from the useful to the absurd.
