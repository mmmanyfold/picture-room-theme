Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ship?

We ship domestic and international orders via UPS Worldwide. If all the items of your order are in stock, we will ship your order within one week. All items shipped via UPS Worldwide will be insured for their full value. All flat or rolled items shipped via Fedex will be insured up to $1000. We ensure your packages are safely packed so that framed artworks arrive to your door in perfect condition.

What is your privacy policy?

We never share your information with anyone.

What is your return policy?

Due to the rare and delicate nature of our artworks, we are unable to accept returns on fine art prints, editions, posters or unique artworks. We do accept book returns. To arrange a return please contact info@mcnallyjacksonstore.com or give us a call during store hours: 212 219 2789.

Where do you source artworks?

We acquire artworks from many sources, mostly from the artists themselves, or from collectors and institutions. If you have any specific questions about the provenance of an artwork on our website, please feel free to contact us by email at info@mcnallyjacksonstore.com, or give us a call during store hours: 212 219 2789.

Do you gift wrap?

We do! For online orders, just include a note to us in the special notes section upon check out. Please tell us what you would like your hand-written note to say to its recipient, and indicate whether you would prefer us not to include the invoice in the package.
